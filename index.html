Motif Discovery with MAP-Elites

Framing DNA motif discovery as a quality-diversity problem to recover multiple high-quality motif hypotheses, compared against standard motif discovery tools under matched criteria.

Alcohol Consumption Phenotyping (MATRR)

ML analysis of alcohol-intake trajectory classifications in nonhuman primates, evaluating stability and predictive validity across modeling approaches.
